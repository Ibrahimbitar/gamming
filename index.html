Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is based on California and Nevada. Players control three protagonists—Michael De Santa, Trevor Phillips, and Franklin Clinton—throughout the course of the game, all of whom have different stories and objectives. The player can also explore the world, complete missions, steal cars, rob banks, buy properties, get involved in gang wars and much more.

FIFA 21 is a football

FIFA 21 is a football simulation video game developed by EA Sports, part of the FIFA series. It is the 28th installment in the FIFA series and was released 9 October 2020 for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Stadia. The game is based on the same Frostbite engine that is used in previous installments of the FIFA series. The game features a variety of game modes, including career mode, Ultimate Team, and Volta Football. Players can create their own team, customize their players' kits, compete against others around the world in online matches, and participate in tournaments. FIFA 21 also features advanced artificial intelligence and improved physics-based gameplay..

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. It was first released in 2009, and has since become one of the most popular video games of all time. Minecraft allows players to build structures using blocks of various materials, explore randomly generated worlds, and battle enemies. Players can also craft weapons, armor, and other items by combining materials. The game provides opportunities for creative expression, as players can create their own unique structures and environments. Additionally, various game modes provide unique challenges and experiences, such as the Survival mode which forces players to manage their resources carefully in order to survive against hostile mobs. Online multiplayer is available, allowing players to connect with each other and cooperate or compete in building structures, adventuring, and more.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is a popular first-person shooter video game franchise developed by Treyarch and Infinity Ward and published by Activision. The series began in 2003 and has since become one of the most successful and recognizable franchises in gaming. It features an expansive single-player story campaign, engaging multiplayer modes, and exciting cooperative experiences. Each entry in the series features new weapons and maps, along with an evolving narrative to bring players into the fight. The franchise also boasts a thriving esports community, with professional tournaments and competitions..

Overwatch

aOverwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One in May 2016. The game features a number of different game modes, with players divided into two teams of six. Each player on a team selects from a roster of various heroes, which each have unique abilities and roles within a team. Players work together to secure and defend control points on a map or escort payloads across the map in a limited amount of time. Players gain cosmetic rewards that do not affect gameplay, such as character skins and victory poses, as they continue to play in matches.
